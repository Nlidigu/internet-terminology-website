introduction


PackNET offers flexible membership plans designed for businesses of all sizes. Whether you are a small startup or a large enterprise, there is a plan that fits your networking needs and budget.

Basic Plan – $299 per month

The Basic Plan is ideal for small businesses with up to fifty users. It includes basic network monitoring, email support with a twenty-four hour response time, monthly security reports, and five gigabytes of cloud storage. This plan gives you essential networking protection at an affordable price.

Professional Plan – $599 per month



The Professional Plan is perfect for growing companies with up to two hundred users. It includes advanced network monitoring, priority email and phone support with a four hour response time, weekly security reports, fifty gigabytes of cloud storage, and quarterly network audits. This plan helps you stay ahead of potential issues before they become problems.
Enterprise Plan – Custom Pricing
The Enterprise Plan is designed for large organizations with complex networking needs. It includes unlimited users, a dedicated twenty-four seven support team, one hour response time for critical issues, real-time security monitoring, unlimited cloud storage, monthly on-site visits, and fully customized solutions built specifically for your business.
What All Plans Include

Every PackNET membership includes free access to all our networking events, discounted training and certification programs, exclusive discounts from our technology partners, priority technical support, and early access to new features before they are released to the public.
How to Sign Up
To become a PackNET member, visit our website and click the Sign Up button. Choose your preferred plan, create your account, and our team will contact you within twenty-four hours to begin the onboarding process.
